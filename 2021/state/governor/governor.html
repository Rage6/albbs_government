POLICIES & GOALS
Gov. Saxton, Lt. Gov. Lerner and their team are dedicated to leading their state in the right direction.
POLICIES
  • Policy 1
    "Operation Pothole": Legalize Marijuana, tax it, and proceed to infrastructure.
  • Policy 2
    Combine Mental Health and Addiction Services under Health-making 1 department head of Health, and two separate sub departments of 1) mental health and 2) addiction services. This ensures that mental health and addiction services do not have to split up funds, but instead they can combine with Health and enjoy one large fund where monies will be allocated fairly and more efficiently.
  • Policy 3
    Establish committee to oversee and investigate possible issues of wasteful government spending, ie vacant positions, non-utilized sections, and unnecessary projects. Ensures that the tax payer dollar is going to use.
  • Policy 4
    Free Market, limited interference economy.
  • Policy 5
    Election Day is a holiday.
GOALS
  • Governors Goal 1
    - To unite the delegates under common causes and discourage gridlock, instead promoting compromise
  • Governors Goal 2
    - To pass a great deal of legislation and carefully record the results of the new policies
  • Governors Goal 3
    - To experiment with the differing sides of the real-life political spectrum, while keeping an open mind throughout the process
  • Governors Goal 4
    - Upon election, the Governor will list his goals in order to provide clear direction to his constituents.
